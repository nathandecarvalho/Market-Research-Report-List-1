<p><p>The ignition spark tester market consists of two types: without plug spark testers and with plug spark testers. Without plug spark testers are devices used to test the ignition spark without removing the spark plug from the engine. They are generally more versatile and can be used on various spark plug types. On the other hand, with plug spark testers require the removal of the spark plug for testing. Although they may offer more accurate results, they are less flexible and specific to certain plug types.</p></p>
