Titanium abutments are durable and biocompatible, steel abutments are strong but can cause allergic reactions, ceramic abutments provide a natural look but are not as strong as metal options, and zirconia abutments are virtually indestructible and aesthetically pleasing. Each type of final abutment offers unique benefits and considerations for patients.</p></p>
