<p><p>Softball cleats are specifically designed footwear for softball players to provide traction and stability on the field. There are two main types of softball cleats based on the ground they are intended for: soft ground and hard ground. Soft ground cleats have longer and more widely spaced studs to penetrate and grip the soft or wet playing surfaces, such as natural grass or muddy fields. On the other hand, hard ground cleats feature shorter studs that are closely spaced to offer better traction on firm and dry playing surfaces, like artificial turf or hard-packed dirt.</p></p>
