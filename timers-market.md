<p><ul><li>Analog Timer</li><li>Digital Timer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Timers are devices used to measure a specific interval of time. Analog timers use mechanical components to measure time, usually displayed through rotating hands or dials. These timers have a traditional, analog look and are often used in various settings like homes, schools, and offices. On the other hand, digital timers utilize electronic components and display time numerically on a digital screen. They are more precise, easier to use, and often equipped with additional features like countdown and alarm functions. Digital timers are commonly found in modern appliances, such as ovens and microwaves.</p></p>
