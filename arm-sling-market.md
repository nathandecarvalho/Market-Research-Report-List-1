<p><p>The arm sling market offers two main types of slings: adjustable arm slings and non-adjustable arm slings. Adjustable arm slings are designed with straps or buckles that can be adjusted to fit the individual's arm size and comfort level. This allows for a customized fit and easy adjustments as needed. On the other hand, non-adjustable arm slings come in pre-set sizes and are not designed to be adjusted. These slings are typically made of a soft material and offer a simple and economical option for supporting and immobilizing the arm.</p></p>
