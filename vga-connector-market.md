<p><p>The VGA connector market can be classified into three types based on cable length: under 2 meters, 2 to 5 meters, and above 5 meters. Under 2 meters refers to VGA cables that are shorter than 2 meters in length and are commonly used for connecting devices in close proximity. The 2 to 5 meters market comprises VGA cables with lengths ranging from 2 to 5 meters, suitable for slightly larger distances. Above 5 meters market includes VGA cables longer than 5 meters, typically used for connecting devices that are further apart, such as in larger conference rooms or auditoriums.</p></p>
