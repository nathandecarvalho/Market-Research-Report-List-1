<p><p>The construction motor grader market consists of two main types: rigid frame motor graders and articulated frame motor graders. Rigid frame motor graders have a single chassis that is fixed in a straight line, providing more stability and control for precise grading operations. On the other hand, articulated frame motor graders have a jointed chassis, allowing for better maneuverability and versatility on uneven terrains. Both types play a significant role in the construction industry, offering different features to meet various grading needs.</p></p>
