<p><p>3D rendering refers to the process of transforming 3D wireframe models into 2D images or animations with the help of sophisticated computer software. One of the key drivers of this market growth is the increasing adoption of 3D rendering solutions in the construction and real estate industries. The use of 3D renderings allows architects, designers, and developers to showcase their projects in a more immersive and photorealistic manner, leading to improved communication and better decision-making.</p><p>Another factor contributing to the growth of the 3D rendering market is the rise of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) technologies. These technologies require high-quality 3D renderings to create immersive and realistic virtual environments. As VR and AR gain popularity across industries, the demand for advanced 3D rendering solutions is also expected to increase.</p><p>Furthermore, the advancements in cloud computing and the availability of powerful rendering software have made 3D rendering more accessible and cost-effective.
