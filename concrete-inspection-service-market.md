<p><p>Concrete inspection service market is categorized into two main types: ultrasonic inspection and manual inspection. Ultrasonic inspection involves the use of high-frequency sound waves to assess the quality and integrity of concrete structures. It helps identify any hidden defects or cracks within the concrete. On the other hand, manual inspection relies on visual examination and physical testing methods to evaluate the condition of concrete, such as checking for cracks, assessing strength, and determining durability. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and longevity of concrete structures.</p></p>
