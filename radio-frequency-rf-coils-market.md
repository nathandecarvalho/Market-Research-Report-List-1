<p><p>The Radio Frequency (RF) Coils market consists of different types, including Volume Coils and Surface Coils. On the other hand, Surface Coils are smaller and used for specific body parts, providing high-resolution imaging. Both types of RF Coils are essential components in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) systems, capturing and transmitting radiofrequency signals for accurate and detailed diagnostics.</p></p>
