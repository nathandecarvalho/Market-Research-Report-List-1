<p><p>A brushless synchronous generator is an electrical machine that converts mechanical energy into electrical energy. It consists of a rotor and a stator, with the rotor being a permanent magnet or an electromagnet. Unlike conventional generators that have brushes and commutators, brushless synchronous generators have an electronic device called an exciter to produce a magnetic field on the rotor.</p><p>The market for brushless synchronous generators has been witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for reliable and efficient power generation systems. These generators are widely used in various industries such as power plants, oil and gas, marine, and aerospace, among others. They offer advantages such as high efficiency, low maintenance, and enhanced durability.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the brushless synchronous generator market is the integration of advanced control systems and digital monitoring capabilities.
