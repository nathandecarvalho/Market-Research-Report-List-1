<p><strong>The Oilfield Roller Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drive Chain</li><li>Conveyor Chain</li><li>Multi Strand Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oilfield roller chain market consists of three different types: drive chain, conveyor chain, and multi-strand chain. A drive chain is used to transmit power between two parallel shafts. It is commonly found in rotary systems, such as drilling rigs. A conveyor chain is designed to convey materials in motion, commonly used in oilfield equipment like conveyors and elevators. Lastly, a multi-strand chain consists of multiple strands, typically used for heavy-duty applications, such as lifting equipment or pumping units in oilfields.</p></p>
