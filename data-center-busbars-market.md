<p><ul><li>3 Phase 4 Wire</li><li>3 Phase 5 Wire</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data center busbars are an integral part of power distribution systems in data centers. The three types of busbars - 3 Phase 4 Wire, 3 Phase 5 Wire, and Others - refer to the wiring configurations used in these systems. 3 Phase 4 Wire busbars distribute power using three phases and a neutral wire, while 3 Phase 5 Wire busbars include an additional grounding wire for improved safety. The category of Others includes alternative wiring configurations that may be customized or unique to specific data center requirements. These busbars facilitate efficient and reliable power distribution within data centers.</p></p>
