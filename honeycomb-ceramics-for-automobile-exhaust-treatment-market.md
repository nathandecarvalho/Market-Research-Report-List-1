<p><p>Honeycomb ceramics are widely used in automobile exhaust treatment due to their high surface area and excellent heat resistance. In the market, there are two main types of honeycomb ceramics: straight-through carrier and wall current carrier. Straight-through carrier honeycomb ceramics have a simple design with straight channels that allow for efficient gas flow. On the other hand, wall current carrier honeycomb ceramics have a more complex design with baffles and walls that help in improving the conversion efficiency of harmful emissions.</p></p>
