<p><p>Compost turners are machinery used in the process of composting to efficiently turn and mix organic waste materials. Drum compost turners have a cylindrical shape and rotate to aerate the compost pile, promoting decomposition. Elevating face compost turners, on the other hand, have a vertical design and lift the compost pile upwards to achieve aeration. These market types cater to different scale and operational requirements, allowing individuals and businesses to select the most suitable compost turner for their composting needs.</p></p>
