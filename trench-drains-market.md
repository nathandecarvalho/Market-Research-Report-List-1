<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Trench drains are categorized into different types based on their construction and installation methods. Cast-in-place trench drains are built on-site using concrete poured directly into a trench. Pre-cast concrete trench drains are manufactured off-site and then transported and installed on location. Liner systems involve adding a liner, usually made of PVC, to an existing trench to create a drain. Former systems use prefabricated form templates to create trenches, allowing for easy installation. These various types offer flexibility and convenience for different construction projects and drainage needs.</p></p>
