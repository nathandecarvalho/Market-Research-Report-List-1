<p><p>The portable gas chromatograph market consists of two main types: gas-liquid chromatographs and gas-solid chromatographs. Gas-liquid chromatographs utilize a liquid stationary phase and a gas mobile phase to separate and analyze chemical compounds. They are commonly used for applications such as environmental testing and food safety. On the other hand, gas-solid chromatographs employ a solid stationary phase and a gas mobile phase to separate and analyze compounds. They are commonly used for applications such as drug analysis and forensic testing. Both types of portable gas chromatographs offer flexibility and efficiency for on-the-go analysis.</p></p>
