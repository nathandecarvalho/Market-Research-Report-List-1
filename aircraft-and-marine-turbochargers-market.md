Twin Turbo includes two turbochargers, providing a more efficient and powerful performance for medium to large-sized aircraft and marine engines. Electro-Assist Turbo integrates an electric component to enhance turbine efficiency, offering improved power and fuel economy in both aircraft and marine applications.</p></p>
