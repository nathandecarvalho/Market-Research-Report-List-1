<p><p>The ultra-high resolution camera market consists of two main types: Area Scan Camera and Line Scan Camera. Area scan cameras capture a complete image of the designated area each time they are triggered, making them suitable for applications where objects are static or moving at a slow pace. On the other hand, line scan cameras capture images by scanning objects line by line, making them ideal for applications involving continuous or high-speed object inspection. Both types are used in various industries including manufacturing, surveillance, and healthcare for their specific imaging needs.</p></p>
