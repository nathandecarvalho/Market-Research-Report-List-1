<p><p>The Medical EHR System market is divided into two main types: Cloud-based and On-premise systems. Cloud-based systems store medical records and information on remote servers accessed through the internet. They offer scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. On-premise systems, on the other hand, store data locally within the healthcare facility. They provide greater control and customization options but require more resources and maintenance. Choosing between the two depends on a healthcare organization's specific needs, security concerns, and budget restrictions.</p></p>
