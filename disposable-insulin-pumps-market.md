Factors such as the increasing prevalence of diabetes, rising awareness about the benefits of insulin pump therapy, and technological advancements in pump devices are driving the market growth. <p><ul><li>Closed-Loop</li><li>Open-Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable insulin pumps are medical devices used by individuals with diabetes to deliver insulin into their body. The closed-loop market refers to disposable insulin pumps that are designed to continuously monitor the individual's blood glucose levels and adjust insulin delivery accordingly, ensuring optimal glucose control. On the other hand, the open-loop market refers to disposable insulin pumps that do not have the ability to automatically adjust insulin delivery based on glucose levels; instead, users manually set their insulin doses. Both types aim to improve diabetes management, but closed-loop pumps offer more automated and personalized control.</p></p>
