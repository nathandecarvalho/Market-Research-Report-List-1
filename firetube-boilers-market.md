<p><p>Firetube boilers are widely used in various industries and are categorized into market types based on technology type and mounting type. Technology type includes wetback and dryback boilers. Wetback boilers have a water-cooled reversing chamber, while dryback boilers have a refractory-lined chamber. Mounting type includes vertical and horizontal boilers. Vertical boilers are compact and space-saving, while horizontal boilers are commonly used in applications requiring high steam capacities. These market types offer diverse options to meet the specific needs of industries and applications.</p></p>
