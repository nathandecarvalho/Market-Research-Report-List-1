<p><strong>The Asphalt Recycling In-situ Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Mix Regeneration</li><li>Cold Mix Regeneration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Asphalt Recycling In-situ Equipment market consists of two main types: Hot Mix Regeneration and Cold Mix Regeneration. Hot Mix Regeneration involves heating the existing asphalt pavement and adding new materials to create a revitalized mixture. This process is commonly used for major road projects. On the other hand, Cold Mix Regeneration does not require heating and relies on emulsifiers and foaming agents to rejuvenate the old asphalt. This technique is usually employed for smaller repairs and maintenance work on road surfaces.</p></p>
