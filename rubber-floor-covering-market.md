<p><p>The rubber floor covering market can be classified into two types: homogeneity rubber floor covering and heterogeneous rubber floor covering. </p><p>Homogeneity rubber floor covering refers to a type of rubber flooring where the composition and properties of the material are consistent throughout the product. It offers a uniform appearance and performance, usually used in industrial or commercial settings where durability and functionality are prioritized.</p><p>Heterogeneous rubber floor covering, on the other hand, consists of multiple layers of rubber materials with different compositions and properties. This type of flooring offers more design options and can mimic the look of other materials such as wood or stone. It is commonly used in residential or commercial settings where aesthetics are as important as functionality.</p></p>
