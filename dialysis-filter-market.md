<p><strong>The Dialysis Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Throughput (Kuf>20mL/h/mmHg)</li><li>Low Throughput (Kuf<10mL/h/mmHg)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dialysis Filter market can be categorized into two types based on their filtration capacity: High Throughput and Low Throughput. High Throughput filters, with a Kuf (ultrafiltration coefficient) greater than 20 mL/h/mmHg, have a higher filtration rate and are capable of removing larger volumes of fluid and solutes during dialysis. On the other hand, Low Throughput filters, with a Kuf less than 10 mL/h/mmHg, have a lower filtration rate and are suitable for patients who require a gentler and slower removal of fluid and solutes during dialysis.</p></p>
