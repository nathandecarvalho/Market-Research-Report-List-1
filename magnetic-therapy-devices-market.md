<p><ul><li>Constant Energy Magnets</li><li>Pulsed Electro-Magnetic Field Energy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic therapy devices market types include constant energy magnets and pulsed electro-magnetic field energy devices. Constant energy magnets are designed to provide a continuous magnetic field to the affected area, promoting healing and relieving pain. On the other hand, pulsed electro-magnetic field energy devices generate varying magnetic fields that pulse at specific frequencies to stimulate cellular activity, improve blood circulation, and reduce inflammation. Both types of devices aim to harness the potential benefits of magnetic fields in promoting overall wellness and alleviating various health conditions.</p></p>
