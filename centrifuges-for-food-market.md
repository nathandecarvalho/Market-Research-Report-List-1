<p><p>Centrifuges for food come in two main market types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical centrifuges are designed to handle large volumes of liquid and separate them based on density. Horizontal centrifuges, on the other hand, are more suitable for solid-liquid separation and are often used in food processing applications like juice, oil, and sugar refining. Both types play a crucial role in the food industry by efficiently separating and clarifying different components to improve product quality and safety.</p></p>
