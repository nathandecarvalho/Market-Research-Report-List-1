<p><p>The handheld camcorder market is divided into several types. Mini-DV camcorders utilize digital tapes to record video footage and are popular among professionals due to their image quality. DVD camcorders record directly onto DVDs, allowing for easy playback on DVD players. Hard Disk Drive (HDD) camcorders store recordings on a built-in hard drive, offering high storage capacity. Flash memory camcorders use removable memory cards for storage, providing flexibility. Combo models combine different types of storage, offering users more flexibility and choice in their recording options.</p></p>
