<p><p>The hand exerciser market can be divided into four types: unadjustable hand exercisers, adjustable hand exercisers, finger unadjustable hand exercisers, and finger adjustable hand exercisers. Unadjustable hand exercisers have a fixed resistance level and are suitable for individuals who don't require variation in resistance. Adjustable hand exercisers allow users to customize the resistance level according to their needs. Finger unadjustable hand exercisers target specific finger muscles without the option to change resistance. Lastly, finger adjustable hand exercisers offer the flexibility to adjust resistance levels while targeting finger muscles for a more personalized workout.</p></p>
