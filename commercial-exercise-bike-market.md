<p><p>The commercial exercise bike market consists of two main types: upright and back-to-back bikes. Upright bikes are similar to traditional bicycles, with a vertical seating position and pedals directly beneath the rider. They provide a more intense workout targeting the lower body muscles. Back-to-back bikes, on the other hand, feature two saddles placed side by side, allowing two users to exercise simultaneously. These bikes offer a more interactive and engaging experience, suitable for group training sessions or interactive competitions.</p></p>
