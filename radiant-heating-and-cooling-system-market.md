<p><p>The radiant heating and cooling system market includes various types such as embedded surface systems, thermally active building systems, capillary surface systems, and radiant panels. Embedded surface systems involve heating or cooling elements embedded within the floors, walls, or ceilings to provide comfortable indoor temperatures. Thermally active building systems use building components like walls or ceilings to provide heating and cooling effects. Capillary surface systems employ microtubes to distribute heated or cooled fluid through the building. Lastly, radiant panels refer to heating or cooling elements installed directly on walls or ceilings to regulate indoor temperatures.</p></p>
