<p><p>Time to Digital Converters (TDC) are electronic devices used to measure the time difference between two events with high precision. On the other hand, the multichannel market offers TDCs with multiple input channels, enabling simultaneous measurement of time intervals from different sources. This type of TDC is commonly used in telecommunications, radar systems, and high-speed data acquisition, where multiple events require precise timing measurements.</p></p>
