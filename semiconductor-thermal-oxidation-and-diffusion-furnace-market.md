<p><p>Semiconductor thermal oxidation and diffusion furnace market types refer to two different furnace designs used in manufacturing semiconductor devices. Horizontal furnaces have a flat configuration, where wafers are placed on a horizontal boat and processed in a horizontal orientation. Vertical furnaces have a vertical orientation, where wafers are typically suspended on a quartz carrier and processed in a vertical direction. Both furnace types are utilized for thermal oxidation and diffusion processes in semiconductor fabrication, but differ in terms of wafer loading, footprint, and efficiency.</p></p>
