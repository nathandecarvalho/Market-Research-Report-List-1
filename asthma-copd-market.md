<p><p>The Asthma & COPD market is divided into three main types: anti-inflammatory drugs, bronchodilator monotherapy, and combination drugs. Anti-inflammatory drugs are used to reduce inflammation in the airways and help prevent asthma attacks or COPD exacerbations. Bronchodilator monotherapy refers to the use of medications that help relax the muscles around the airways, making it easier to breathe. Combination drugs combine both anti-inflammatory and bronchodilator agents in a single medication, providing relief for both inflammation and bronchospasms in one treatment.</p></p>
