<p><p>The car buffer polishers market is divided into three main types: orbital polishers, dual-action polishers, and others. Orbital polishers are known for their circular motion which helps in evenly spreading the polish and reducing the risk of paint damage. Dual-action polishers are popular for their simultaneous rotation and oscillation movements, providing better control and minimizing the chances of swirl marks. The "others" category includes both rotary polishers and pneumatic polishers, which are commonly used by professionals due to their higher power and efficiency in removing deep scratches and defects.</p></p>
