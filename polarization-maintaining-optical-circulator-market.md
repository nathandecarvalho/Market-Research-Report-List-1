<p><p>The Polarization Maintaining Optical Circulator Market is segmented into two types: Three Port and Quad Port. A Three Port Optical Circulator is designed to route light from Port 1 to Port 2 while blocking light from Port 2 to Port 1. It also allows the transmission of light from Port 1 to Port 3. On the other hand, a Quad Port Optical Circulator routes light from Port 1 to Port 2, Port 2 to Port 3, Port 3 to Port 4, and Port 4 to Port 1. These circulators enable efficient transmission and manipulation of light signals in various applications.</p></p>
