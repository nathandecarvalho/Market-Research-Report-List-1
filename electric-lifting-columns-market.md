<p><p>Electric lifting columns are devices used for vertical movement in various applications. The market for electric lifting columns can be classified into two types: multi-stage electric lifting columns and two-stage electric lifting columns. </p><p>Multi-stage electric lifting columns are designed with multiple stages, allowing for a greater vertical range. This makes them suitable for applications where a wide range of height adjustment is required.</p><p>On the other hand, two-stage electric lifting columns have two stages, providing a more limited vertical range. They are generally used in applications that do not require as much height adjustment.</p><p>Both types of electric lifting columns offer efficient and reliable vertical movement, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
