<p><p>Micro-total analytical system (µTAS) is a promising technology for various applications in biomedical and environmental fields. It employs microfluidic channels and electrodes for performing analytical operations. On the other hand, the EFAD chip, short for electrochemical flow-assisted deposition, combines microfluidic devices with electrochemical sensing techniques for efficient detection of trace analytes. Both these market types deliver miniature, portable, and automated systems for rapid and sensitive analysis.</p></p>
