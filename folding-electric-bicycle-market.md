<p><p>A folding electric bicycle, also known as an e-bike, is a type of bicycle that combines the convenience of a folding mechanism with the power of an electric motor. This can be attributed to several factors, including the growing popularity of e-bikes as a sustainable and eco-friendly mode of transportation.
