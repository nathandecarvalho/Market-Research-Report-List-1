<p><p>Orthopaedic imaging equipment market comprises various types of imaging systems used in the diagnosis and treatment of orthopaedic conditions. X-ray systems utilize electromagnetic radiation to produce images of bones and joints. CT-scanners provide detailed cross-sectional images using X-rays and computerized processing. MRI systems use magnetic fields and radio waves to generate high-resolution images. EOS imaging systems enable 3D imaging with low radiation exposure. Ultrasound systems use sound waves to create images of soft tissues. Nuclear imaging systems employ radioactive substances to capture images of bone structure, metabolism, and blood flow.</p></p>
