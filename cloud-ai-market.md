<p><p>Public Clouds in the Cloud AI market are accessible to the general public, where AI services are provided by third-party vendors through the internet. Private Clouds, on the other hand, are dedicated infrastructures exclusively for a single organization, giving them control and security over their AI systems. Hybrid Clouds combine both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to use a mix of both for their specific AI needs. Each market type offers distinct advantages, enabling businesses to choose the most suitable model for their AI deployment.</p></p>
