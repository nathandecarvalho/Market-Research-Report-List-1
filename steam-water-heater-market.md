<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The steam water heater market is categorized into three types: instantaneous steam water heater, semi-instantaneous steam water heater, and storage steam water heater. Instantaneous steam water heaters quickly heat water as it passes through, providing hot water on demand. Semi-instantaneous steam water heaters store a limited amount of hot water and continuously replenish it to meet demand. Storage steam water heaters store a large volume of hot water and maintain it at a constant temperature until it is needed.</p></p>
