<p><p>Phytoestrogen supplements are categorized into two main types: flavonoids and non-flavonoids. Flavonoids are a type of phytochemical found in various plants, such as soybeans and red clover. These compounds have been associated with potential health benefits, including the ability to mimic estrogen in the body. On the other hand, non-flavonoids encompass a variety of other plant compounds, like lignans and coumestans, found in sources like flaxseed and legumes. Both flavonoids and non-flavonoids are commonly utilized in phytoestrogen supplements, which aim to provide individuals with additional sources of estrogen-like substances.</p></p>
