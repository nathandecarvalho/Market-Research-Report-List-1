<p><p>The hybrid plastic railroad ties market is divided into two types: polyurethane (PU) and high-density polyethylene (HDPE). On the other hand, HDPE railroad ties are manufactured from a high-density plastic material that provides excellent strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types offer advantages in terms of low maintenance costs, reduced environmental impact, and extended lifespan compared to traditional wooden ties in the railway industry.</p></p>
