<p><p>Electro-Optical Switches are devices used to control the transmission of optical signals. Single-mode switches are designed for use with single-mode fibers, which have a narrow core diameter and are used for long-distance communication. They provide high-quality and low-loss signal transmission. On the other hand, multimode switches are used with multimode fibers, having a larger core diameter and used for short-distance communication. Multimode switches offer cost-effective solutions with lower transmission quality but higher data rates. Both types cater to different optical network requirements and have distinct market segments.</p></p>
