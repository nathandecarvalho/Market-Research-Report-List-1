<p><p>Combustion Process Management Systems consist of two main types: Burner Management Systems and Boiler Control Systems. Burner Management Systems ensure the safe operation of burners by monitoring factors like flame detection and fuel flow, preventing potential accidents. On the other hand, Boiler Control Systems oversee the overall operation of boilers, maintaining optimal boiler performance by adjusting factors such as temperature, pressure, and fuel supply. Both types contribute to the efficient and safe functioning of combustion processes in various industries, enhancing productivity and minimizing risks.</p></p>
