<p><strong>The Reticle POD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EUV</li><li>Non EUV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Reticle POD (Pellicle Open Dome) market can be categorized into EUV and non-EUV segments. EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) Reticle PODs are designed to protect photomasks used in EUV lithography systems, which enable more precise and advanced chip manufacturing. Non-EUV Reticle PODs, on the other hand, are suitable for conventional lithography systems that use optical light sources. Both market segments address different needs in the semiconductor industry, with EUV Reticle PODs catering to the latest technology advancements and non-EUV Reticle PODs serving existing manufacturing processes.</p></p>
