The Angiogenesis Activation Assay Kit is used to detect and measure the activation of angiogenesis, which is the process of forming new blood vessels. On the other hand, the Angiogenesis Inhibition Assay Kit is used to evaluate and study compounds or agents that can inhibit angiogenesis, preventing the formation of new blood vessels. Both types of assay kits are essential tools in understanding and studying angiogenesis-related processes.</p></p>
