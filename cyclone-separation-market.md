Hydrocyclones are used in industries such as mining and oil and gas, and they employ centrifugal force to separate solids from liquids. Gas cyclones, on the other hand, are used in industries like power plants and refineries, and they utilize swirling gas flow to separate particles from a gas stream. Both types of cyclones play a crucial role in various industries by efficiently separating particles for different purposes.</p></p>
