<p><p>The Multi-Mode Fiber Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) market includes two main types: Single-end Configuration and Loop Configuration. In Single-end Configuration, the optical fiber is connected to one end of the DTS system, allowing temperature measurements along the entire length of the fiber. On the other hand, Loop Configuration involves connecting the two ends of the DTS fiber together, forming a loop. This enables continuous temperature monitoring without the need for any external devices. These configurations offer flexibility and accuracy in monitoring temperature across various applications.</p></p>
