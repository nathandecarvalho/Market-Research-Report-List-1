<p><p>Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) is a type of polyethylene used in various applications due to its toughness and chemical resistance. Two common market types for MDPE production are the Autoclave Process and Tubular Process. The Autoclave Process involves the use of high pressure and temperature in a reactor to produce MDPE, while the Tubular Process uses a series of interconnected tubes to polymerize ethylene into MDPE. Both processes have their own advantages and are widely used in the production of MDPE.</p></p>
