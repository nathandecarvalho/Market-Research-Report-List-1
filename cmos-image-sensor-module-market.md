<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CMOS Image Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI)</li><li>BSI Stacked</li><li>Front-illuminated (FI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS Image Sensor Module market is segmented into three types based on the technology used. First, the Backside-Illuminated (BSI) sensors have their photodiodes at the back, allowing better light sensitivity. Second, BSI Stacked sensors have an added layer for image processing, resulting in faster and more efficient performance. Lastly, Front-Illuminated (FI) sensors have their photodiodes at the front, limiting their light sensitivity but offering higher resolution. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences, providing options for manufacturers and consumers in the CMOS Image Sensor Module market.</p></p>
