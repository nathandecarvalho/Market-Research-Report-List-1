<p><p>The Ultrasound Probe Disinfection Market consists of various types of probes that require disinfection to ensure patient safety and maintain probe functionality. </p><p>1. Linear Probe: Used for imaging shallow structures, it uses high-frequency sound waves transmitted in a straight line.</p><p>2. Convex Probe: Suitable for abdominal imaging, it uses lower frequency sound waves transmitted in an outward-curved direction.</p><p>3. Phased Array Probe: Ideal for cardiac imaging, it produces sound waves in a phased manner to create detailed images.</p><p>4. TEE Probe: Specifically designed for transesophageal echocardiography, it allows high-quality imaging of the heart.</p><p>5. Others: Refers to any other type of ultrasound probe that requires disinfection to prevent cross-contamination and maintain proper hygiene.</p></p>
