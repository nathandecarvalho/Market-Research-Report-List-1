<p><strong>The Push Notification Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Premises</li><li>Cloud Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Push notification software market includes two types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises push notification software is installed and operated on the user's local servers, offering greater control and security but requiring manual maintenance and updates. Conversely, cloud-based push notification software is hosted on a remote server and accessed through the internet, providing ease of scalability and automatic updates but relying on a reliable internet connection. Organizations can choose the option that best suits their requirements, depending on their preference for control, security, and infrastructure management.</p></p>
