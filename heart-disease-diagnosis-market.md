<p><p>Heart disease diagnosis market includes various types of diagnostic methods such as ECG (Electrocardiogram), blood tests, angiography, and computerized cardiac tomography. ECG is a non-invasive test that records electrical activity of the heart. Blood tests measure various biomarkers to identify heart-related problems. Angiography is an invasive test that uses contrast dye and X-rays to visualize blood flow in the heart. Computerized cardiac tomography combines X-ray technology and computer processing to create detailed images of the heart. These diagnostic methods play a crucial role in identifying and diagnosing various heart diseases.</p></p>
