<p><p>Fog detectors are devices used to detect the presence of fog, which helps in various applications like aviation, transportation, and safety systems. There are two main types of fog detectors: hardwired detectors and battery-operated detectors. Hardwired detectors are connected to a power source and provide continuous monitoring. They are commonly used in permanent installations. On the other hand, battery-operated detectors rely on batteries for power and are more portable, making them suitable for temporary or mobile deployments. Both types serve the purpose of detecting fog but differ in terms of power source and mobility.</p></p>
