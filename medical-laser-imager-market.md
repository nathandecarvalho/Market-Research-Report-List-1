<p><p>The medical laser imager market can be classified into two types: traditional medical laser imagers and self-help medical laser imagers. Traditional medical laser imagers are typically larger, more complex, and require professional expertise for operation. They are commonly found in hospitals and diagnostic centers, offering high-resolution printing of medical images. In contrast, self-help medical laser imagers are smaller, portable devices that can be operated by individuals themselves. These are commonly used at home or in small clinics, providing convenient and immediate access to medical image printing.</p></p>
