<p><p>A prepaid credit card is a payment card that is loaded with a predetermined amount of money by the cardholder before making any purchases. Prepaid credit cards are convenient for those who do not have a bank account or for individuals who want to control their spending.</p><p>The prepaid credit card market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue the same trajectory in the coming years.
