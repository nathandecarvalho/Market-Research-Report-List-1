<p><strong>The Ski Helmets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PC Shell</li><li>ABS Shell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ski helmets market is divided into two main types: PC shell and ABS shell. PC shell helmets are made from a polycarbonate material that offers high impact resistance and durability. They are lighter in weight and provide better protection against severe head injuries. On the other hand, ABS shell helmets are made from a thermoplastic material that provides good impact resistance and lower cost. They are slightly heavier than PC shell helmets but offer sufficient protection for recreational skiing. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and safety requirements.</p></p>
