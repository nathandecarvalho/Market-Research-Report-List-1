<p><p>The dermal curette market consists of two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable curettes are designed for single-use and are made from materials that are intended to be discarded after each procedure. They offer advantages in terms of sterility, convenience, and preventing cross-contamination. Reusable curettes, on the other hand, are designed to be sterilized and used multiple times. They are made from durable materials that can withstand repeated sterilization processes. Reusable curettes provide cost savings over time and are a more environmentally friendly option.</p></p>
