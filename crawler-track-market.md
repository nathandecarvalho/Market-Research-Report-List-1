<p><p>The crawler track market has two main types: rubber tracks and steel tracks. Rubber tracks are made from synthetic materials and are commonly used in compact construction machinery and agricultural equipment. They offer excellent traction, low ground pressure, and reduced vibrations, making them suitable for various terrain types. Steel tracks, on the other hand, are often employed in heavy-duty applications like mining and forestry. They provide superior durability and stability but may cause more surface damage and are less suitable for delicate terrains. Both track types offer specific benefits depending on the intended use.</p></p>
