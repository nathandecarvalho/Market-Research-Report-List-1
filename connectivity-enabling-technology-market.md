<p><p>Connectivity enabling technologies refer to various wireless communication protocols that allow devices to connect and communicate with each other. Bluetooth is a short-range wireless technology commonly used for connecting devices like smartphones and headphones. Wi-Fi enables high-speed wireless internet access for devices within a specific area. ZigBee is a low-power wireless technology mainly used for IoT devices and home automation. Other connectivity enabling technologies include NFC (Near Field Communication), which allows devices to exchange data by touching or being close to each other, and RFID (Radio Frequency Identification), used for tracking and identification purposes.</p></p>
