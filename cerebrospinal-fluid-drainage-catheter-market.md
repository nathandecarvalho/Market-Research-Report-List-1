<p><p>The Cerebrospinal Fluid Drainage Catheter market consists of two main types: semi-transparent and fully-transparent catheters. Semi-transparent catheters have a partially see-through design, allowing healthcare professionals to monitor the flow and drainage of cerebrospinal fluid. On the other hand, fully-transparent catheters are completely see-through, providing a clearer view of the fluid flow. Both types play a crucial role in managing conditions related to abnormal cerebrospinal fluid build-up, such as hydrocephalus, by allowing medical practitioners to assess and regulate the drainage process effectively.</p></p>
