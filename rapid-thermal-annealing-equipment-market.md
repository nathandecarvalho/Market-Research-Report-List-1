<p><p>Rapid Thermal Annealing (RTA) equipment market can be categorized into two types: lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based RTA equipment utilizes lamps as a heat source to anneal semiconductor materials at high temperatures, promoting faster and more efficient annealing processes. On the other hand, laser-based RTA equipment employs laser beams for heating the materials, offering precise and controlled annealing. These two types of equipment serve the same purpose of quickly annealing materials, however, they differ in the heat source employed, leading to variations in their performance and efficiency.</p></p>
