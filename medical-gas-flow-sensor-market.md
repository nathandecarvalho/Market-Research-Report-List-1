<p><p>The medical gas flow sensor market consists of two main types: flange gas flow meter and plug-in gas flow meter. A flange gas flow meter is a type of sensor that is installed by attaching it to a pipe through a flange connection. This allows for accurate measurement and monitoring of gas flow in medical applications. On the other hand, a plug-in gas flow meter is a sensor that can be easily plugged into a gas pipeline, providing a convenient and portable solution for measuring gas flow rates in medical settings. Both types of sensors are crucial in ensuring the safe and efficient delivery of medical gases.</p></p>
