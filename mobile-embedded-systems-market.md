<p><ul><li>Embedded Hardware</li><li>Embedded Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile embedded systems market consists of two main types: embedded hardware and embedded software. Embedded hardware refers to the physical components integrated within mobile devices, such as processors, memory, sensors, and other electronic parts. It is responsible for the device's basic functionalities and operation. On the other hand, embedded software pertains to the programs, codes, and operating systems specifically designed for mobile devices. It enables the device to run applications, manage resources efficiently, and provide a user-friendly interface. Both embedded hardware and software are crucial for the overall functionality and performance of mobile devices.</p></p>
