<p><p>Closed tracheal suctioning system is a medical device used to remove secretions and mucus from the lungs and airways of patients who are unable to cough effectively. It consists of a closed system with a suction catheter that is inserted into the trachea to suction out the secretions. This closed system helps prevent the entry of contaminants into the patient's airway, reducing the risk of infection and complications.</p><p>The closed tracheal suctioning system market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and asthma, is one of the key factors driving market growth. These conditions often result in excessive production of secretions in the airways, necessitating the use of closed tracheal suctioning systems for effective clearance.</p><p>Advancements in technology and design of closed tracheal suctioning systems are also contributing to market growth. Manufacturers are focusing on developing innovative, user-friendly devices that provide accurate and efficient suctioning while minimizing discomfort to the patient.
