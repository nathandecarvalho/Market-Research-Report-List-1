<p><p>The robotic packaging arm market can be classified into several types based on the number of axes they operate on. A 2-axis robotic arm can move in two directions, typically in a linear or rotational motion. Similarly, a 3-axis arm can move in three directions, commonly used for pick and place tasks. A 4-axis arm adds an additional rotational axis, while a 5-axis arm introduces the ability to tilt or swivel. A 6-axis arm offers the maximum freedom of movement, combining all the previous axis capabilities. Apart from these, there are also other miscellaneous robotic packaging arm types, which may have unique or specialized capabilities suited for specific applications.</p></p>
