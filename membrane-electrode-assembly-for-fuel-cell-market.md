<p><p>The Membrane Electrode Assembly (MEA) for Fuel Cell market is segmented into three types: 3-layer MEA, 5-layer MEA, and Others. The 3-layer MEA is composed of a central proton exchange membrane (PEM) sandwiched between two catalyst-coated gas diffusion layers (GDLs). In the 5-layer MEA, it consists of an additional microporous layer (MPL) and an electrode under the GDL. The Others category includes MEAs with unique compositions or designs. These different types offer varying performance and efficiency levels, providing options for fuel cell manufacturers based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
