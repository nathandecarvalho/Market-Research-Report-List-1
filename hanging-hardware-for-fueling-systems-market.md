<p><ul><li>Whiphose</li><li>Breakaway</li><li>Coaxial Hose</li><li>Nozzle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hanging hardware for fueling systems refers to various components used in the fuel dispensing process. Whiphose is a flexible hose that connects the fuel dispenser to the vehicle's fuel tank. Breakaway is a safety device installed between the hose and nozzle, disconnecting them in case of excessive force or accidental drive-off. Coaxial hose incorporates an inner and outer hose to remove static electricity. Nozzles are handheld devices that control the fuel flow and are equipped with automatic shut-off to prevent overfilling. These hardware types are commonly used in fueling systems to ensure safe and efficient fuel dispensing.</p></p>
