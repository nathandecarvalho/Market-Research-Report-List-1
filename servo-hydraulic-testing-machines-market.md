<p><p>Servo-hydraulic testing machines are used in various applications such as tension, compression, flexure, fatigue, fracture mechanics, damping properties, and vibration testing. Tension testing involves applying force to a material to determine its strength and elasticity. Compression testing assesses the ability of a material to withstand compressive forces. Flexure testing measures a material's bending strength. Fatigue and fracture mechanics testing evaluates a material's durability and resistance to failure. Damping properties testing assesses a material's ability to dissipate energy. Vibration testing measures a material's response to oscillating forces.</p></p>
