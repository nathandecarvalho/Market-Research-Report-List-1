<p><p>Ventricular Assistance Devices are mechanical pumps used to support heart function in patients with heart failure. There are various types of VADs including Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVAD), Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVAD), and Bi-ventricular Assist Devices (BiVAD). RVADs help pump blood from the right ventricle to the lungs, LVADs assist in pumping blood from the left ventricle to the rest of the body, and BiVADs support both sides of the heart. These devices are crucial in improving quality of life and extending survival for patients awaiting heart transplantation.</p></p>
