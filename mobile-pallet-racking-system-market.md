<p><p>The Mobile Pallet Racking System market consists of three types: Manual Control Pallet Racking, Remote Control Pallet Racking, and Automatic Control Pallet Racking. In the Manual Control type, operators physically move the racks to access goods. Remote Control type allows operators to remotely control the movement of racks through a control panel. Automatic Control type uses advanced technology, such as sensors and software, to automatically move the racks without human intervention. These systems offer different levels of efficiency and convenience, catering to various industrial storage needs.</p></p>
