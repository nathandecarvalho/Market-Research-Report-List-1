<p><p>The Automated Passenger Counting System (APCS) market offers various types of technologies for accurately counting passengers in different transport modes. Infrared technology uses sensors that detect body heat to count individuals boarding or exiting a vehicle. Stereoscopic vision utilizes two cameras to create a 3D image that helps count passengers. Time-of-flight systems measure distance with light, providing accurate passenger numbers. IP cameras rely on video analytics to track passenger movement. All these technologies aim to improve transportation efficiency, optimize resources, and enhance passenger safety and experience.</p></p>
