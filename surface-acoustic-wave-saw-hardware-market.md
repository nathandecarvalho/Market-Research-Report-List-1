<p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Hardware Market encompasses various types of components. Filters are used to eliminate unwanted signals or to allow specific frequencies to pass. Correctors adjust the amplitude or phase of signals. Converters transform signals from one form to another. Delay lines introduce a time delay in a signal. Oscillators generate continuous waveforms. Other components include transducers, resonators, and sensors. Each type of SAW hardware serves specific functions in telecommunications, radar systems, and electronic devices, improving signal quality, transmission, and processing capabilities.</p></p>
