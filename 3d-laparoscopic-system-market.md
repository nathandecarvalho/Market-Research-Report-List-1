<p><p>The 3D laparoscopic system market includes two main types: flexible laparoscopes and rigid laparoscopes. Flexible laparoscopes are thin and flexible instruments that allow for better maneuverability within the body during surgery. They offer a wider field of view and are suitable for complex procedures. Rigid laparoscopes, on the other hand, are straight and have a fixed viewing angle. They are ideal for basic surgeries and provide high-quality images. Both types contribute to the advancement of minimally invasive surgeries and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
