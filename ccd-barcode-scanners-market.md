<p><p>CCD barcode scanners are available in two main types: wired and wireless. Wired scanners are connected to a computer or terminal through a physical cable, providing a reliable and consistent connection. They are suitable for fixed or stationary setups where mobility is not a concern. On the other hand, wireless scanners use Bluetooth or radio frequency technology to communicate with the computer or terminal, offering greater flexibility and mobility. They are ideal for applications that require a scanning range without the constraint of physical wires.</p></p>
