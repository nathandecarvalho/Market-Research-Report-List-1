<p><p>High barrier type retort pouches are commonly divided into two types based on their structural design: stand-up pouches and flat pouches. Stand-up pouches have a gusset at the bottom that allows them to stand upright on store shelves, making them visually appealing and convenient for consumers. Flat pouches, on the other hand, lie flat when filled and are often used for products that do not need to stand upright. Both types provide high barrier protection, prolonging the shelf life of the packaged products.</p></p>
