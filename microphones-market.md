<p><p>Microphones are classified into two broad types: wireless microphones and wired microphones. Wireless microphones rely on radio waves or infrared signals to transmit audio signals without the need for physical cables. They offer freedom of movement and flexibility but tend to be more expensive. On the other hand, wired microphones are connected to the audio source through cables. These are more affordable and offer reliable signal transmission, although they limit movement. Both types have their unique advantages and are widely used in various settings, such as performances, public speaking, and broadcasting.</p></p>
