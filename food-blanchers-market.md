<p><p>Food blanchers are equipment used in the food processing industry to heat or steam food products for a short period of time before further processing. Belt blanchers have a conveyor belt system that moves the food through a chamber with hot water or steam. Drum blanchers consist of a rotating drum where food is placed and heated through direct contact with hot water or steam. Screw blanchers use a screw mechanism to transport the food product, exposing it to hot water or steam. These different types of blanchers offer various benefits and are used based on the specific food processing needs.</p></p>
