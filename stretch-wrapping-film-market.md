<p><p>Stretch wrapping film is a highly stretchable plastic film that is used to secure and stabilize loads on pallets during transportation and storage. The increasing demand for efficient and cost-effective packaging solutions to protect products during transit is driving the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the stretch wrapping film market is the adoption of nano-layered films. These films are designed to provide enhanced strength, puncture resistance, and load containment capabilities, thereby improving overall packaging efficiency.
