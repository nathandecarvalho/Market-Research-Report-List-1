<p><p>The medical suction system market is categorized into two types: portable and non-portable. They provide efficient and immediate suction capabilities in emergency situations. Non-portable suction systems, on the other hand, are more robust and designed for permanent installation in healthcare facilities such as operating rooms and intensive care units. They offer higher suction power and can handle larger volumes of fluid and debris.</p></p>
