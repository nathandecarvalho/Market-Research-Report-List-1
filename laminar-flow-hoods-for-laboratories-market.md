<p><p>Laminar flow hoods for laboratories are specialized equipment used to create a controlled environment for conducting sensitive experiments or handling hazardous materials. There are two main types of laminar flow hoods: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal laminar flow hoods have clean air flow moving horizontally from the back towards the front, while vertical laminar flow hoods have clean air flow moving vertically from top to bottom. These hoods are designed to prevent the contamination of samples or experiments, ensuring a sterile and safe working environment for laboratory personnel.</p></p>
