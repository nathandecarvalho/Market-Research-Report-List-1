<p><p>The automatic assembly machines market consists of two main types, fully automatic assembly machines and semi-automatic assembly machines. Fully automatic assembly machines are designed to perform all assembly tasks without operator intervention, using advanced robotic systems and software. On the other hand, semi-automatic assembly machines require some level of operator involvement during the assembly process. While both types of machines are used in various industries for enhancing productivity and efficiency, fully automatic assembly machines offer higher levels of automation and are better suited for large-scale production environments.</p></p>
