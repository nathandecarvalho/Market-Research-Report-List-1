<p><p>The Wireless Concrete Sensor market consists of two main types: Bluetooth Transmission and Network Transmission. Bluetooth Transmission involves sensors that use Bluetooth technology to transmit data wirelessly and directly to a receiving device such as a smartphone or tablet. On the other hand, Network Transmission involves sensors that are integrated into a network, enabling them to transmit data to a centralized monitoring system over a Wi-Fi or cellular network. Both types offer convenient and efficient methods for monitoring concrete structures in real-time without the need for physical connections.</p></p>
