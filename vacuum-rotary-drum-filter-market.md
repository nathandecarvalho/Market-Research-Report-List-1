<p><p>A Vacuum Rotary Drum Filter (VRDF) is a reliable and versatile equipment used for solid-liquid separation in various industries, including mining, chemical, pharmaceutical, food and beverage, and wastewater treatment. It consists of a rotating drum covered with a filter cloth, which is partially submerged in the slurry or suspension. As the drum rotates, the vacuum inside the drum withholds the filtrate, leaving behind the solid particles on the cloth, which are then discharged.</p><p>The VRDF Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
