<p><p>The Direct Copper-Plated Ceramic Substrate market consists of two main types: Al₂O₃ Ceramic Substrate and AlN Ceramic Substrate. Al₂O₃ Ceramic Substrate refers to a copper-plated ceramic substrate made from aluminum oxide. It is known for its high thermal conductivity, electrical insulation properties, and resistance to corrosion. On the other hand, AlN Ceramic Substrate is a copper-plated ceramic substrate made from aluminum nitride. It boasts superior thermal conductivity and mechanical strength, making it suitable for various applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
