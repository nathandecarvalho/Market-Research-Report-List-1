<p><p>The Air Atomizing Nozzles Market consists of three main types: Flat Spray Nozzles, Full Cone Nozzles, and Hollow Cone Nozzles. Flat Spray Nozzles produce a flat, wide spray pattern, making them suitable for coating or cleaning applications. Full Cone Nozzles emit a circular spray pattern in a full cone shape, used for tasks like gas cooling or dust suppression. Hollow Cone Nozzles eject liquid in a cone shape with a hollow center, making them ideal for tasks requiring maximum liquid coverage, such as humidification or fire protection.</p></p>
