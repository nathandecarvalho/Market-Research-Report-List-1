<p><p>Fogless mirrors are available in two main market types: stable and portable. Stable fogless mirrors are typically installed permanently on walls and require mounting hardware. They are commonly found in bathrooms or dressing rooms. On the other hand, portable fogless mirrors are designed for easy relocation and can be carried around. They often have suction cups or stands for placement on different surfaces. Portable fogless mirrors are convenient for travel, use in multiple rooms, or even outdoor activities. Both types of fogless mirrors serve the purpose of providing a clear reflection without fogging up due to steam or humidity.</p></p>
