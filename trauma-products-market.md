<p><p>Trauma products refer to medical devices used for the treatment of fractures and injuries. Internal fixators are a type of trauma product that includes implants like plates, screws, and rods, which are surgically placed inside the body to stabilize bones during the healing process. External fixators, on the other hand, are devices that are applied externally and use pins or wires to stabilize the bones. These products provide support, alignment, and immobilization, promoting proper healing and recovery in patients with traumatic bone injuries.</p></p>
