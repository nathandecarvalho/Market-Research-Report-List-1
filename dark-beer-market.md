<p><p>Dark beer market types can be classified into two categories: Pasteurized beer and draft beer. On the other hand, draft beer is unpasteurized and delivered directly from the keg to the consumer, offering a more fresh and authentic taste experience. While pasteurized beer is more widely available in bottles and cans, draft beer is commonly enjoyed on tap in bars, restaurants, and pubs.</p></p>
