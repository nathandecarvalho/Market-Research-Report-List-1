<p><p>Turf cutters can be classified into two main types: Mounted Turf Cutters and Walk-behind Turf Cutters. Mounted Turf Cutters are machines that are attached to larger vehicles or agricultural equipment, such as tractors or trucks. They are designed to be more efficient for larger-scale turf cutting projects, such as in agricultural or landscaping applications. On the other hand, Walk-behind Turf Cutters are smaller, self-propelled machines that are operated manually. They are typically used for small-scale turf cutting projects, such as in residential or small commercial areas.</p></p>
