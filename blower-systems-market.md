<p><p>Blower systems are categorized into two main types: vacuum blower systems and air blower systems. Vacuum blower systems are designed to create a negative pressure or suction in order to remove air or gases from a specific area or process. On the other hand, air blower systems are used to generate positive pressure or airflow to push air or gases into a designated space. Both types of blower systems serve various industrial applications, such as wastewater treatment, pneumatic conveying, and mechanical ventilation, among others.</p></p>
