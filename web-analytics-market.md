<p><ul><li>On-Demand</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Web analytics market can be categorized into two types: On-Demand and On-Premise. On-Demand refers to web analytics solutions that are cloud-based, accessible through the internet and offered as a service by third-party providers. This type offers flexibility and scalability, as the data is stored remotely and can be accessed from anywhere. On-Premise, on the other hand, involves web analytics software that is installed and maintained locally on a company's own servers. This type provides more control and security over the data, but requires dedicated IT resources for management and maintenance.</p></p>
