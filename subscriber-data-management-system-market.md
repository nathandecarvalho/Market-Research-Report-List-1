<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Subscriber Data Management System market consists of two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, allowing for scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises solutions are installed and operated within an organization's own infrastructure, providing complete control over data and giving higher security. Both options have their pros and cons, with cloud-based offering easier deployment and maintenance, while on-premises providing better data control but requiring higher upfront costs and IT resources.</p></p>
