<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Unit</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrosurgery Unit market consists of two main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units and Bipolar Electrosurgical Units. Monopole Electrosurgical Units use a single electrode to deliver electrical current for cutting or coagulation during surgery. They are often used in procedures that require deep tissue cutting. On the other hand, Bipolar Electrosurgical Units use two electrodes to control the electrical current and are commonly used for procedures that require precise tissue sealing. Both types of units play a vital role in various surgical procedures.</p></p>
