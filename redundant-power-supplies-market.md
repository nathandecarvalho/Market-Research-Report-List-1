<p><strong>The Redundant Power Supplies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1+1 Power Configuration</li><li>2+1 Power Configuration</li><li>2+2 Power Configuration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Redundant power supplies are used in various industries to ensure uninterrupted power supply and prevent system downtime. The "1+1 power configuration" refers to a setup where two power supplies are connected in parallel, with one serving as the primary source and the other as a backup. The "2+1 power configuration" involves three power supplies, two of which work in parallel to support the system, while the third acts as a redundant backup. Lastly, the "2+2 power configuration" employs four power supplies, two of which are active at a time, and the other two serve as redundant backups, thereby enhancing reliability and power efficiency.</p></p>
