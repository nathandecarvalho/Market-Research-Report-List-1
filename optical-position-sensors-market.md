<p><p>Optical position sensors are devices that measure the position or displacement of an object. The market consists of three main types. One-dimensional optical position sensors measure linear movement along a single axis, such as in printing or robotics applications. Two-dimensional optical position sensors measure position in two axes, allowing for X and Y movement detection, commonly used in medical imaging and computer mice. Multi-axial optical position sensors measure position in multiple axes, enabling detection of complex movements, like in gaming controllers or aerial drones.</p></p>
