<p><p>Angular velocity transducers are devices used to measure the rate of rotation of an object. They are available in three different market types: Single Axis, Dual Axis, and Three-Axis. Single Axis transducers can measure rotation in only one direction, while Dual Axis transducers can measure rotation in two perpendicular directions. Three-Axis transducers have the ability to measure rotation in three mutually perpendicular directions. These transducers find applications in various industries, such as aerospace, automotive, and robotics, where precise measurement of angular velocity is crucial for performance evaluation and control.</p></p>
