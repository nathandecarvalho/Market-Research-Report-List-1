<p><p>The snow sweeper truck market can be categorized into two types: rotary snow sweeper trucks and wedge snow sweeper trucks. Rotary snow sweeper trucks are equipped with spinning brooms that effectively clear snow from roads and surfaces. They are efficient in removing snow and debris. On the other hand, wedge snow sweeper trucks use a wedge-shaped plow blade to push the snow aside, clearing the path. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in snow removal, providing efficient solutions for keeping roads and surfaces clear during snowy conditions.</p></p>
