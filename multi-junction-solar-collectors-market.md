<p><p>The multi-junction solar collectors market consists of two main types: concentrating type collectors and non-concentrating type collectors. Concentrating type collectors use lenses or mirrors to direct and concentrate sunlight onto a smaller area of solar cells, increasing their efficiency. On the other hand, non-concentrating type collectors absorb sunlight over a larger area without concentrating it, making them more suitable for applications where space is not a constraint. Both types have their own advantages and applications, contributing to the overall growth of the multi-junction solar collectors market.</p></p>
