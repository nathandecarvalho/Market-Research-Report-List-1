<p><p>The semiconductor lithography equipment market can be categorized into various types based on the technology used. KrF (krypton fluoride) lithography, I-Line lithography, ArF (argon fluoride) dry lithography, EUV (extreme ultraviolet) lithography, and ArF immersion lithography are the major market segments. KrF and I-Line use ultraviolet light for exposure, while ArF dry uses a higher wavelength of ultraviolet light. EUV lithography employs a much shorter wavelength of light for more precise patterning, and ArF immersion lithography uses a combination of light and liquid immersion to further enhance resolution and accuracy in chip manufacturing.</p></p>
