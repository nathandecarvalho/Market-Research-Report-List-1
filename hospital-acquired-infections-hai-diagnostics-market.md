<p><p>Hospital Acquired Infections (HAI) Diagnostics refer to the medical devices, tests, and methods used to detect and diagnose infections acquired by patients during their stay in healthcare facilities. Furthermore, the increasing use of automated systems for HAI diagnostics is reducing the turnaround time and improving efficiency.</p><p>Another emerging trend in the market is the development of point-of-care (POC) diagnostic tests.
