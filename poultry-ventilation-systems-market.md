<p><ul><li>Natural air Flow System</li><li>Mechanical Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Poultry ventilation systems are designed to maintain a suitable environment for poultry production. The natural air flow system utilizes natural airflow and openings in the poultry house for ventilation, minimizing the need for mechanical equipment. On the other hand, mechanical ventilation systems involve the use of fans, air inlets, and controllers to regulate airflow and maintain an optimal environment. Both systems aim to provide adequate ventilation, temperature control, and air quality for the well-being and productivity of the poultry.</p></p>
