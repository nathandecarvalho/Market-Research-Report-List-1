<p><strong>The Industrial Ball Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flanged Ball Valves</li><li>Threaded Ball Valves</li><li>Socket-Weld Ball Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial ball valves market offers three main types of valves: flanged ball valves, threaded ball valves, and socket-weld ball valves. Flanged ball valves are designed for high-pressure applications and feature flanges for easy installation. Threaded ball valves have threaded ends, making them suitable for small-scale and low-pressure systems. Socket-weld ball valves are ideal for high-temperature and high-pressure environments, as they are welded directly to the piping system. These different types cater to diverse industrial needs and ensure efficient fluid control in various settings.</p></p>
