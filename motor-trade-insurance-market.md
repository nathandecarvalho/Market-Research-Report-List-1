<p><p>Motor Trade Insurance is a type of insurance that provides coverage for businesses involved in the motor trade industry. There are three main types of motor trade insurance: Third Party, Third Party Fire and Theft, and Comprehensive.</p><p>1. Third Party Insurance: This type of coverage protects the policyholder against claims made by third parties involved in an accident caused by the policyholder. It covers damage to other vehicles or property and any injury caused to others.</p><p>2. Third Party Fire and Theft Insurance: This type of policy includes the protection offered by third party insurance and also covers the insured's vehicle if it is stolen or damaged by fire.</p><p>3. Comprehensive Insurance: This is the highest level of coverage and includes protection for the policyholder's own vehicle, in addition to third party claims. It covers damage caused by accidents, theft, fire, vandalism, and other perils.</p></p>
