<p><p>The Veterinary & Animal Vaccines Market consists of various types of vaccines used to prevent and control diseases in animals. Live attenuated vaccines are made from weakened forms of the virus or bacteria. Inactivated vaccines contain killed microorganisms. Toxoid vaccines contain inactivated toxins produced by bacteria. Recombinant vaccines are made by inserting genetic material into a harmless virus or bacteria. Other vaccines include vector vaccines and DNA vaccines. These vaccines play a crucial role in safeguarding the health and well-being of animals by stimulating their immune systems to fight off diseases.</p></p>
