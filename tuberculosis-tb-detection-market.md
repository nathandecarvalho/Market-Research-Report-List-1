<p><p>The Tuberculosis (TB) Detection Market includes several types of diagnostic tests. The Bacterial Culture Test involves taking a sample and allowing bacteria to grow in a laboratory to detect TB. The Tuberculin Test, also known as the Mantoux test, determines if a person has been infected with TB bacteria by injecting a small amount of tuberculin protein into the skin and observing the reaction. Rapid Molecular Diagnosis uses advanced techniques to identify the genetic material of TB bacteria quickly. Other types of tests include Serological and Nucleic acid amplification tests, which detect antibodies or specific DNA/RNA sequences related to TB.</p></p>
