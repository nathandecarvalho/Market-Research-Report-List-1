<p><p>The Robotic Gripper System market consists of two main types: Electric Grippers and Pneumatic Grippers. Electric Grippers are powered by electricity and provide precise control and automation in gripping objects. They are commonly used in industries where accuracy and sensitivity are crucial, such as electronics and automotive manufacturing. On the other hand, Pneumatic Grippers use compressed air to generate gripping force. They are known for their quick response time and high gripping force, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications like material handling and assembly in industries such as logistics and packaging.</p></p>
