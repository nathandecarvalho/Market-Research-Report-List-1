Wet suit locks are designed to be used in environments where doors are exposed to moisture, such as bathrooms or outdoor areas. They are constructed with waterproof materials to prevent rust and corrosion. On the other hand, dry suit locks are suitable for regular indoor use, as they are not specifically designed to withstand moisture. They are made with non-waterproof materials, making them less expensive than wet suit locks.</p></p>
