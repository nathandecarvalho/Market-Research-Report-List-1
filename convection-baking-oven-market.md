<p><p>The convection baking oven market is divided into two main types: electric convection ovens and gas convection ovens. Electric convection ovens use electricity as their power source and utilize a fan to evenly distribute heat throughout the oven cavity. Gas convection ovens, on the other hand, rely on natural gas or propane as their power source. They also employ a fan to circulate the heated air, ensuring consistent baking results. Both types of ovens offer efficient and even cooking, with the choice between electric and gas depending on individual preferences and available resources.</p></p>
