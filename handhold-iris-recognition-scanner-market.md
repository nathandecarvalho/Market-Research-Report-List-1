<p><p>The Handhold Iris Recognition Scanner market can be divided into two types: Optical Iris Scanner and Electric Iris Scanner. An Optical Iris Scanner works by capturing detailed images of the iris using light, and then it uses advanced algorithms to identify and authenticate the individual. On the other hand, an Electric Iris Scanner utilizes an electrical current to create a map of the iris, which is then converted into a digital template for identification purposes. Both types aim to provide secure and accurate iris recognition technology for various applications.</p></p>
