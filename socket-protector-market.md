<p><p>The Socket Protector market can be categorized into two types: With Cover and Without Cover. With Cover socket protectors are equipped with a protective covering that can be opened or closed to safeguard the electrical socket when it's not in use. This cover prevents accidental contact or insertion of objects into the socket, reducing the risk of electrical shocks. On the other hand, Without Cover socket protectors do not come with a protective covering, and they mainly provide insulation to prevent foreign objects from entering the socket.</p></p>
