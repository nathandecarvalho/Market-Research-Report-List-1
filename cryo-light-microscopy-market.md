<p><p>Cryo light microscopy market includes two types: manual and automatic. Manual cryo light microscopy involves the use of equipment operated by individuals. In contrast, automatic cryo light microscopy utilizes advanced technology and algorithms to automatically adjust settings, focus, and capture images without human intervention.
