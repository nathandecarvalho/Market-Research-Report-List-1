<p><p>The Lacrimal Duct Stent Tube market consists of two main types: Monocanalicular and Bicanalicular Lacrimal Duct Stent Tube. Monocanalicular stents are designed to fit into a single canaliculus, which is a small duct in the eye that helps drain tears. Bicanalicular stents, on the other hand, are designed to fit into both canaliculi. These stents are used to treat conditions such as blockages or obstructions in the lacrimal ducts, allowing tears to properly drain from the eye.</p></p>
