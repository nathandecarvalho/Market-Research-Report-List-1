<p><ul><li>Hollow</li><li>Rigid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drive shaft market is divided into two main types: hollow and rigid. Hollow drive shafts are characterized by a central hollow tube, which reduces weight without compromising strength. This type is commonly used in lightweight and high-performance vehicles. On the other hand, rigid drive shafts consist of a solid shaft without any hollow space. They are known for their durability and ability to withstand heavy loads, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications such as trucks and industrial machinery. Both types cater to different needs in the market, offering various advantages based on the specific requirements of the vehicle or machinery.</p></p>
