<p><p>The multifunction patient monitor market consists of various types of devices used for monitoring different aspects of a patient's health. Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices monitor the electrical activity of the heart. Event monitors are wearable devices used to record and analyze heart rhythm abnormalities over a longer period of time. Implantable Loop Recorders (ILR) are small devices implanted under the skin to continuously monitor heart rhythm. Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) devices measure the effectiveness of heart function. Additionally, there are other types of monitors that cater to different aspects of patient health.</p></p>
