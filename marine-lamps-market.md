Several factors are driving the growth of this market. Firstly, the increasing naval budgets of various countries across the world are leading to the construction of new naval vessels, thereby boosting the demand for marine lamps. Additionally, the growing marine tourism industry, especially in regions like Asia-Pacific and the Caribbean, is driving the demand for recreational boats and yachts, subsequently augmenting the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, stringent regulations regarding maritime safety and the need for reliable and efficient lighting solutions in the marine industry are further fueling the demand for marine lamps. This shift towards LED-based marine lamps is expected to create lucrative opportunities for market players.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing focus on the development of intelligent marine lamps that incorporate advanced technologies such as sensors, wireless connectivity, and automation.
