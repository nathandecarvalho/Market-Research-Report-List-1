<p><p>Lithotripsy refers to a medical procedure used to break down kidney stones into smaller pieces for easier elimination. There are three types of lithotripsy market: Extracorporeal Lithotripsy (ESWL) involves using shock waves outside the body to break down stones; Intracorporeal Lithotripsy involves passing a laser or ultrasound device through the urinary tract to break the stones; Percutaneous Lithotripsy requires inserting a tube into the kidney to break and remove stones. These different types offer alternative methods for doctors to treat patients with kidney stones.</p></p>
