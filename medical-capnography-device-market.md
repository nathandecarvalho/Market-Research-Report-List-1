<p><p>Medical Capnography Device is a medical device used to measure the level of carbon dioxide (CO2) in a patient's breath during a surgical or medical procedure. Capnography is considered an essential tool for monitoring patients undergoing sedation, during and after anesthesia, and in intensive care units.</p><p>The Medical Capnography Device Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period.
