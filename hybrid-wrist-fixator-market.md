<p><p>The hybrid wrist fixator market offers two main types of fixators: round and L shape. Round fixators are circular in shape and comprise pins or wires that are inserted into the wrist bones, providing stability and support for bone healing. On the other hand, L shape fixators have a bent or angled design, resembling the letter L. These fixators are also inserted into the bones, but their shape allows for better alignment and stabilization, particularly in complex or severe wrist fractures. Both types are used in wrist surgeries to aid in bone repair and recovery.</p></p>
