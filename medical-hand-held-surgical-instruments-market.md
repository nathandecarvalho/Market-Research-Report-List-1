<p><p>Medical hand-held surgical instruments market includes forceps, retractors, dilators, graspers, scalpels, and others. Forceps are used for grasping tissues, while retractors are used to hold back tissues during surgery. Dilators are used to enlarge openings in blood vessels or other tubular structures. Graspers are used to hold and manipulate tissues. Scalpels are used for making precise incisions. Other instruments in this market include scissors, needle holders, and surgical probes. These instruments are crucial in various surgical procedures for improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
