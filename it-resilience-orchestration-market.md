<p><p>The IT resilience orchestration market consists of two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based orchestration is the approach where the IT infrastructure and services are managed over the internet through remote servers. It offers scalability, flexibility, and reduced maintenance costs. On the other hand, on-premises orchestration is when the infrastructure and services are managed within the organization's premises.
