Rigid Urology Endoscopes are straight and inflexible, allowing for direct visualization and precise control during procedures. Flexible Urology Endoscopes, on the other hand, have a flexible shaft that can be maneuvered through the curved urinary tract, providing better access and reduced patient discomfort. Both types play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating urological conditions.</p></p>
