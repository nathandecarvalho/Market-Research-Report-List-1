<p><strong>The Industrial Board-to-Board Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-hole Technology</li><li>Surface-mount Technology</li><li>Plug-in Technology</li><li>Solderless Stacking Mezzanine Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Board-to-Board Connector market consists of different types, each offering unique features for connecting electronic components. Through-hole Technology involves inserting connector pins into holes on a circuit board, ensuring sturdy connections. Surface-mount Technology allows connectors to be directly soldered onto the board's surface, offering a smaller form factor. Plug-in Technology enables connectors to be easily plugged and unplugged. Solderless Stacking Mezzanine Technology allows components to be stacked without soldering, simplifying assembly and maintenance. These various options cater to different industrial needs.</p></p>
