<p><p>Smart home devices are electronic devices that are connected to the internet and can be controlled remotely, allowing homeowners to automate and control various functions within their homes. Smart thermostats, smart lighting, and smart plugs are some of the popular energy management devices in the market.</p><p>In addition, consumers are increasingly adopting smart assistants, such as Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant, which allow them to control various smart devices through voice commands.
