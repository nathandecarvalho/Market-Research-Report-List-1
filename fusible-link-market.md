<p><p>The Fusible Link market consists of two main types: Mechanical Fusible Link and Electrical Fusible Link. Mechanical Fusible Links function by employing a heat-sensitive element that melts when exposed to high temperatures, thus triggering a mechanical action like shutting off a valve. On the other hand, Electrical Fusible Links are designed to break an electrical circuit under excessive current conditions, preventing damage or potential fire hazards. These two types of Fusible Links serve crucial roles in different applications where temperature or current control is necessary to ensure safety and protection.</p></p>
