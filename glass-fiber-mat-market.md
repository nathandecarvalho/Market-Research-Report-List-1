<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass fiber mat is a versatile material used in various industries. It is available in different types based on the manufacturing process. The wet-laid process involves forming a mat by dispersing glass fibers in water and then draining and drying it. This type of mat offers excellent strength and absorbency, making it suitable for applications like filtration and insulation. On the other hand, the dry-laid process involves arranging glass fibers using mechanical or pneumatic methods. This results in a mat with lower absorbency but higher strength, making it ideal for applications like reinforcement in composites and construction materials.</p></p>
