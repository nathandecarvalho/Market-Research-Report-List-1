<p><p>Butter powder is available in two types: salted butter powder and unsalted butter powder. Salted butter powder contains added salt, which enhances the flavor and makes it suitable for various savory dishes. On the other hand, unsalted butter powder does not have any additional salt, allowing for more control over the salt content in recipes. Both types of butter powder are convenient to use and offer a long shelf life. They are commonly used as a flavoring and topping for popcorn, baked goods, sauces, and other culinary applications.</p></p>
