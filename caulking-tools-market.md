<p><p>The caulking tools market can be classified into three types. Manual caulking tools are operated by hand and are suitable for small-scale projects. Air caulking tools utilize compressed air to dispense caulking material and are ideal for large-scale or professional projects. Electric caulking tools are powered by electricity and offer a more efficient and precise caulking process, making them convenient for both small and large-scale projects. These three types cater to different needs and preferences in the caulking industry.</p></p>
