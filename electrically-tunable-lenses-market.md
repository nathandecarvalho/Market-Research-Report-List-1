<p><p>The electrically tunable lenses market consists of two types: flat convex and flat concave lenses. A flat convex lens has a flat surface on one side and a convex surface on the other. It can be modified electronically, allowing the user to adjust the focal length, thereby changing the lens' ability to focus. A flat concave lens, on the other hand, has a flat surface on one side and a concave surface on the other. Similar to a flat convex lens, it can be electrically tuned to modify its focal length and alter the focusing capabilities.</p></p>
