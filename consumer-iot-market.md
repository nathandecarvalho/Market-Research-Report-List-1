<p><p>Consumer IoT market is composed of four main types: IoT Node Component, IoT Network Infrastructure, Internet of Things Solutions, and Internet of Things Services. </p><p>IoT Node Component refers to the physical devices like sensors and actuators that collect data or perform actions. IoT Network Infrastructure comprises the technologies and protocols that enable communication and connectivity between these devices. Internet of Things Solutions are software applications and platforms that enable users to control and monitor their IoT devices. Internet of Things Services involve third-party companies that offer services like data analytics, security, and maintenance for the IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
