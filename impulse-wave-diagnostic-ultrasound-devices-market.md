<p><p>Impulse wave diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized into three types: A Type, M Type, and B Type. A Type devices are used for abdominal imaging and provide real-time images with high resolution. M Type devices are utilized for imaging moving organs, such as the heart, and can capture the motion. B Type devices are designed for general imaging and display a 2D image in real-time. Each type has specific applications and capabilities, catering to various diagnostic needs in the medical field.</p></p>
