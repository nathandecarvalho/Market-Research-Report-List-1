<p><p>Lithium starters are divided into two main market types: standalone and plug-in. Standalone lithium starters are independent systems that are installed directly onto the engine, providing the required power to start it. On the other hand, plug-in lithium starters are connected to an external power source, such as an electrical outlet, to charge the lithium battery before starting the engine. Both types offer convenience and reliability, but the plug-in market allows for easier recharging and potentially longer battery life due to external power sources.</p></p>
