<p><p>Coagulation tests are used to assess the blood's ability to form clots, which is important in various medical conditions. The market for these tests can be categorized into four main types based on the technology used: mechanical, electrochemical, optical, and other technologies. Mechanical tests involve assessing the time it takes for blood to clot using instruments like the shear-based devices. Electrochemical tests utilize electrodes to measure changes in electrical signals during clotting. Optical tests use light to detect clot formation. Other technologies refer to emerging and alternative approaches for coagulation testing.</p></p>
