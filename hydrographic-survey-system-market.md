This information is crucial for a variety of applications including navigation safety, charting, resource exploration, and environmental monitoring.</p><p>The hydrographic survey system market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for accurate and up-to-date hydrographic data to support maritime activities is a major driver of market growth. These systems are used to map the seabed and identify potential drilling locations.
