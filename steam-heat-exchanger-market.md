<p><p>The steam heat exchanger market consists of two main types: direct heating equipment and indirect heating equipment. Direct heating equipment refers to heat exchangers that come into direct contact with the substance being heated, such as steam directly transferring heat to water. Indirect heating equipment, on the other hand, utilizes a separate heat transfer medium, such as a thermal oil or water, to transfer heat from the steam to the substance being heated. Both types are widely used in various industries for efficient and effective heating applications.</p></p>
