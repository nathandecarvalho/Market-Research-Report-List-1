<p><p>Valves and manifolds are key components in various industries. Valves are devices that regulate the flow of fluids or gases by opening, closing, or partially obstructing a passage. They are used to control the flow rate, pressure, and direction of these substances. Manifolds, on the other hand, are interconnected systems that distribute fluids or gases from a single source to multiple outputs. They facilitate the control and distribution of these substances within a system. Both valves and manifolds play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and safety of industrial processes.</p></p>
