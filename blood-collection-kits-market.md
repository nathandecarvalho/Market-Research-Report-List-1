<p><ul><li>Venous Blood Collection Kits</li><li>Capillary Blood Collection Kits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blood collection kits are essential tools used in medical settings for obtaining blood samples for diagnostic testing and monitoring purposes. There are two main types of blood collection kits: venous blood collection kits and capillary blood collection kits. Venous blood collection kits are used to draw blood from veins, typically from the arm, using a needle and a vacuum tube system. Capillary blood collection kits, on the other hand, collect blood through a small prick in the fingertip or heel, using a lancet and a collection device like a capillary tube or microcontainer.</p></p>
