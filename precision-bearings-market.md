<p><p>Precision bearings are specialized mechanical components used in various industries to support rotational and linear movements with minimal friction and high accuracy. Ball bearings consist of balls between two raceways, while roller bearings utilize cylindrical or tapered rollers. Ball bearings are typically suitable for lighter load applications, higher speeds, and low friction, while roller bearings are more adept at handling heavy loads and shock absorption. Both types find extensive use in diverse sectors such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery, enhancing efficiency and reducing wear and tear.</p></p>
