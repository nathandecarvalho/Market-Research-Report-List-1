<p><ul><li>Reflective</li><li>End to End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear smoke detectors are widely used in various industries and buildings to detect the presence of smoke and prevent potential fire hazards. There are two main types of linear smoke detector markets: reflective and end to end. Reflective smoke detectors use a transmitter and a receiver placed opposite each other to detect smoke when it blocks the beam of light between them. On the other hand, end to end smoke detectors have the transmitter and receiver placed at either end of the space, detecting smoke when it interrupts the beam of light between them. These types of smoke detectors assist in ensuring early fire detection and prompt action.</p></p>
