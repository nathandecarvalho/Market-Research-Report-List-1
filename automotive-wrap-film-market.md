<p><strong>The Automotive Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Wrap Film is a type of material used to cover and protect the exterior of vehicles. There are two main types: Cast Film and Calendered Film. Cast Film is made by pouring a liquid mixture onto a casting sheet, creating a thin and flexible film with enhanced durability, conformability, and resistance to fading. On the other hand, Calendered Film is produced by extruding and calendering a hot resin material, resulting in a thicker and less expensive film. However, it may not have the same durability or ability to conform to complex surfaces as Cast Film.</p></p>
