<p><p>Fingerprint chips market consists of three main types: Capacitive Fingerprint, Optical Fingerprint, and Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module. Capacitive Fingerprint technology uses electrical current to create an image of the fingerprint. Optical Fingerprint technology uses light to capture a digital image of the fingerprint. Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module technology uses ultrasound waves to map the fingerprint's unique features. Each type of fingerprint chip has its own advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as accuracy, speed, and security requirements.</p></p>
