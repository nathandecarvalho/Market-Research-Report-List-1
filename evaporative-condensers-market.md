First, coil tube type condensers consist of a coil tube that contains refrigerant, which is cooled by circulating air. Plate type condensers, on the other hand, use plates to transfer heat and condense the refrigerant. Lastly, vertical type condensers are built in a vertical configuration, with air flowing vertically through the condenser. These types offer different advantages and are chosen based on specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
