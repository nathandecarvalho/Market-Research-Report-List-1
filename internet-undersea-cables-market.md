<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The internet undersea cables market can be classified into two primary types: unrepeatered cable and repeatered cable. Unrepeatered cables are non-amplified and can transmit data over shorter distances. They rely on the optical properties of the fiber to carry signals. Repeatered cables, on the other hand, have built-in amplification devices called repeaters, allowing them to transmit signals over longer distances. These repeaters amplify the optical signals to maintain their strength and quality, ensuring efficient data transmission across vast oceanic distances.</p></p>
