<p><p>The plaster mortar market comprises three main types: premixed mounting mortars, premixed rush coats, and premixed plasters. Premixed mounting mortars are used for fixing tiles, stones, and other materials to walls and floors. Premixed rush coats are applied before the final plaster to create a smooth and even surface. Premixed plasters, on the other hand, are used as the final coating for interior and exterior walls. These plaster mortar types play a crucial role in construction by providing strong adhesion, durability, and aesthetic appeal to various surfaces.</p></p>
