<p><p>The backlight module market consists of two main types: CCFL backlight module and LED backlight module. CCFL backlight modules use cold cathode fluorescent lamps to emit light, while LED backlight modules utilize light-emitting diodes. Both types are commonly used in the display industry to provide uniform and efficient backlighting for various devices like TVs, laptops, and smartphones. However, LED backlight modules have gained popularity due to their lower power consumption, slim design, longer lifespan, and ability to achieve better color reproduction compared to CCFL backlight modules.</p></p>
