<p><p>Rigid sleeve couplings are mechanical devices used to connect two shafts together in order to transmit power. They come in three main types: elastomeric couplings, mechanical couplings, and metallic couplings. Elastomeric couplings are made of rubber or polymer materials and provide flexibility and shock absorption. Mechanical couplings use mechanical components like gears or levers to transmit torque. Metallic couplings are made of metal materials and offer high torque transmission capabilities. These different types of couplings cater to various applications and industries based on their specific advantages and requirements.</p></p>
