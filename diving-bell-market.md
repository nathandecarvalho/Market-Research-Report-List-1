<p><ul><li>Wet Bell</li><li>Closed Bell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diving bell market can be categorized into two main types: wet bell and closed bell. Wet bells are open-bottomed structures used primarily for shallow-water diving. It allows the divers to operate while being partially submerged in water, providing them with direct access to the underwater environment. On the other hand, closed bells are airtight chambers primarily used for deep-sea diving. These chambers are pressurized to match the ambient pressure at the diving depths and typically include life support systems for the divers.</p></p>
