<p><p>Sealed gauge pressure sensors are used to measure pressure in various applications. These sensors are classified into three types based on the pressure range they can measure: low pressure, mid pressure, and high pressure. </p><p>Low pressure sensors are designed to accurately measure pressure in the lower range, generally below 10 psi. Mid-pressure sensors can measure pressure between 10 and 1000 psi. High pressure sensors, on the other hand, are capable of measuring pressure above 1000 psi. These different types cater to the specific needs of different industries and applications requiring pressure measurement.</p></p>
