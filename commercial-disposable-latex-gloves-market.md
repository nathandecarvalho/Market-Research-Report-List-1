<p><ul><li>Powdered Gloves</li><li>No Powdered Gloves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial disposable latex gloves market consists of two types: powdered gloves and no powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a fine cornstarch powder that absorbs moisture, making them easier to put on and take off. They are commonly used in medical and food service industries. On the other hand, no powdered gloves do not have any coating and are preferred in industries where powder contamination is a concern, such as the electronics or automotive industry. These gloves offer superior tactile sensitivity but may be slightly harder to don and doff.</p></p>
