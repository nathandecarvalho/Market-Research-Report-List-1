<p><ul><li>Serum Separating Tubes</li><li>EDTA Tubes</li><li>Plasma Separation Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood collection products market consists of various types of tubes used for collecting blood samples for diagnostic purposes. Serum separating tubes are designed to separate serum (liquid portion of blood) from the other components. EDTA tubes contain Ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid, a substance that prevents blood from clotting. Plasma separation tubes help separate plasma (the liquid component containing proteins) from blood cells. These different types of tubes are used in laboratories and healthcare facilities for accurate and efficient blood sample collection, storage, and analysis.</p></p>
