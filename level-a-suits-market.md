<p><p>Level A suits are protective garments used in hazardous environments. There are two types of market entry in Level A suits: front entry and rear entry. Front entry suits feature zippers located at the front, allowing for easy donning and doffing. They are suitable for environments where workers need quick access to the suit. Rear entry suits, on the other hand, have zippers positioned at the back. These suits provide a more secure closure and are commonly used in situations where contaminated suits need to be removed carefully to prevent exposure.</p></p>
