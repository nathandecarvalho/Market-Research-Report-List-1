<p><p>The surgical instrument lubricant spray market comprises two main types: silicone-based and non-silicone based. Silicone-based lubricants are typically derived from a combination of polymers and silicones, providing excellent lubrication and protecting instruments from corrosion. On the other hand, non-silicone based lubricants do not contain any silicone compounds. These lubricants are often made from biocompatible oils or water-based solutions and offer a safer alternative for sensitive surgical procedures or instruments where silicone usage is restricted. Both types serve the important function of reducing friction and facilitating the smooth operation of surgical instruments.</p></p>
